Enghouse Aculab, Decades of innovation

For over 40 years, Enghouse Aculab has been providing a range of essential telecoms products to the global communications market. Our technology is used to deliver voice, fax and data solutions for use within IP, PSTN and mobile networks with performance levels that are second to none.
