Making sales easy for charity retailers

The Kudos EPoS system makes sales so easy for volunteers in-store because the touch screen is designed and tailored to each user.

Our experience of hundreds of charity and hospice shop system implementations across the UK means we know that minimising the need to press or find buttons, or select from drop-down lists, keeps your volunteers smiling and helps to streamline the sales process. By keeping software simple, the Kudos charity retail system enables volunteers to focus on engaging with customers and donors in-store, resulting in happy customers.

The business intelligence you can glean from the software solution is accurate and timely to help charity retailers make the most of opportunities and identify potential issues before they have any impact.

Maximising Gift Aid revenue for the charity sector

By automating and simplifying Gift Aid claims, our solution helps the charity retail sector reduce hidden costs and administrative burdens, freeing up valuable time for your team to focus on your mission.

Unlocking the full potential of Retail Gift Aid is essential for charity shops looking to raise more funds and make a greater impact. Our charity EPoS system is purpose-built to streamline the Gift Aid process, enabling charity retailers to claim Gift Aid donations with ease and boost Gift Aid revenue on every eligible sale.

With an easy-to-use interface and an intuitive touchscreen till system, volunteers and staff can quickly process donated items, manage donations, and track sales data in real-time. This seamless donation process ensures that every Gift Aid opportunity is captured, while accurate sales figures and stock levels are always at your fingertips.

Real-time charity EPoS system reporting

Real-time reporting and business intelligence tools provide head office and store teams with instant access to sales data, stock levels, and Gift Aid performance. This empowers charity retailers to make informed decisions, optimise their sales process, and identify new opportunities to raise funds.

By joining our tailored charity EPoS solution, UK charities can take control of their sales, manage donated goods more effectively, and deliver a better experience for both donors and customers. With improved efficiency, enhanced supporter management, and accurate reporting, your charity can focus on what matters most—delivering your mission and making a difference in your community.
