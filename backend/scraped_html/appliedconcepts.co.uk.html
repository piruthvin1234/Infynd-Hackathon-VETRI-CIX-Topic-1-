About

Based in Cambridge at the heart of the UK’s world-leading life sciences sector, Applied Concepts helps start-up or established clients in medtech, biotechnology and industrial fields to develop innovative and robust embedded systems that will power tomorrow’s state of the art devices.


Early stage concept development

With broad expertise in both electronics hardware and software, we specialise in transforming ideas into proof of concept prototypes.


Technologies and tools

  • Hardware design – Analog and digital.
  • In-vitro diagnostics, microfluidics control, mechatronics.
  • Video capture and transmission.
  • Arm MCU, FPGA.
  • C, MISRA-C, C++, C#.
  • IEEE 802.11 WiFi, BTLE.
  • MQTT IoT.
  • Hard Real-time : Bare metal / RTOS (VxWorks, μC/OS-II, Free RTOS).
  • Test-Driven design. Static analysis.
  • Industry standard tools : Linux, Windows, IAR, Altium, git, Jira, CUnit, Azure DevOps, TeamCity.
