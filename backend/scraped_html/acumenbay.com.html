About Us

About us

Acumen Bay Consultancy is an outcome focused and a result driven IT organisation with an aim to improve client satisfaction, their customers experience by creating value in their businesses operations.  We provided end to end services across the entire IT application lifecycle. We engage as early as ideation to deployment while quickly progressing through pilot phases using our rapid action team. We cover a broad range of commercial-off-the-shelf product implementation or bespoke development using the latest technologies while keeping the data at the heart of our delivery framework to ensure maximum benefits to clients

Our Approach

Our service includes a comprehensive consultation to help identify gaps and opportunities, an assessment report that includes the overall strategy, operational plan, cost-benefit  analysis, and a schedule. We also offer a suite of quality products through our partner organizations to accelerate your delivery while engaging with your business teams in a highly collaborative and agile environment

Why Us?

We will bring right resources who  aim to improve client satisfaction, their customers experience by creating value in their businesses. We strive to help businesses develop a solid foundation and business strategy to provide them long term sustainability and competitive edge. We will work to improve sales, drive productivity, governance, and an organisation which is flexible to global changes.
