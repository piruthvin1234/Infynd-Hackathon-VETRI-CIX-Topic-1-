Transforming Processes

What Do We Do?

We provide a continually evolving, intuitive and configurable SaaS solution that helps organisations manage business rules, transform processes, optimise workflows, and deliver high-quality and efficient services to their clients.

Our solution - creatiogreen - supports a variety of organisations offering both regulated or non-regulated training, courses, products or qualifications such as:

  • Awarding Organisations (AOs)

  • Membership and Professional Bodies

  • Charities

  • Public Sector Bodies

  • End-Point Assessment Organisations (EPAOs)

  • Private Companies

Each organisation can choose which modules they require and configure them according to their needs. These modules can be used individually or seamlessly integrated with one another. These include:

  • Product Development

  • Customer Management

  • Register, Assess & Award

  • Governance

  • Learner Portal

  • End-Point Assessment

Tailored to Your Needs

creatiogreen can be configured to meet a wide range of requirements, whether you need:

  • Covering all your development, delivery and awarding activities - helping you to meet and evidence relevant regulatory requirements.

  • To help transform the way you engage and support learners throughout their learner journey. Empowering them to self-start and manage a wide range of activities - including the ability to share their achievements with external organisations.

  • To support your qualification development, launch and ongoing management activities.

  • To provide you with an immediate profile and picture of each customer, underpinning the delivery of your products and services, which in doing so streamlines various workflows and processes.

  • That goes much further than simply registering and awarding learners. Our LMS offers a range of intuitive features such as supporting assessment activities, sampling and moderation, plus so much more...

  • To help you manage, quality-assure, inspect, investigate and risk-rate customers, as well as moderating and sampling their assessment activities, and so much more...

  • Which supports and spans a range of internal governance areas, including: board & committee meetings, business continuity plans, risk management, self-assessments, internal/external audits, managing information assets, conflicts of interests, suppliers and much more...

  • To help underpin and evidence your approach to complying with various regulatory requirements. It embeds and enforces different regulatory rules or external accreditation schemes into your configured workflows, processes, forms and logs.
