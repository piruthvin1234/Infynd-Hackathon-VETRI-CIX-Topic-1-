The OSP Ethos

Educate

Achieved through a variety of accredited online and classroom training at various levels including Awareness and Practitioner courses and exercises.

Protect

Practical advice and consultancy from board room to server room on security and data protection. Integrating by design, by default into your companies strategy. External advice, assurance and auditing of governance, risk management and capability for data protection and security can be provided.

Enable

Enable innovation and business growth in the digital age, through holistic data protection and security by design and by default to meet legal and business requirements.
