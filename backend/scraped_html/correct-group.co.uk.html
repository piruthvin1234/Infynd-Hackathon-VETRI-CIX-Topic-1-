    Key Industries We Work With:

    IT support for Charities is different. That’s because vendors provide not-for-profit pricing options that make solutions more affordable for them. 
    Correct Group have helped our Not For Profit clients (NFPs) obtain discounted software and to meet particular issues for charities, such as the reassurance of Security and Anonymity of user data.

    The Construction industry utilises many areas of technology, including CAD software (which uses especially large file sizes) and remote working sites (that require distributed networks). Specialised Software typically used by these clients includes AutoCad, Costex, Kato and Union Square, amongst many others.   

    Correct Group has worked extensively with agencies and we know from experience that creative minds need creative support. Images and Videos are inherently large file types requiring storage solutions, faster switches and different backup ideas. Apple Mac users need to know that their systems will talk with the rest of the office. We have a dedicated Apple team just for you!
